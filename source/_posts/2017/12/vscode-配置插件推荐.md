## background

一款美化插件配置如下：

```js
"background.useDefault": false,
    "background.enabled": true,
    "background.customImages": [
        "file:///Users/mac/Desktop/improve/1.jpg"
    ],
    "background.style": {
        "opacity": 0.1,
        "width": "1600px",
        "height": "1150px"
    }
```

> ps: 超级超级吃 CPU 慎用！！
